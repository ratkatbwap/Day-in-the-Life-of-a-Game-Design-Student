Assignment for Computational Arts 2026.

Walk through a 3D model of my house and go through my morning routine of collecting my things, eating breakfast and petting my rabbit.
Walk up to the collectable objects (the laptop, the bag and the chips) to collect them. Collected items are kept track of on the top left corner of the screen.
Walk to the back door (blue double doors in the kitchen) to begin the 2D rabbit minigame, where you must press space ten times to complete it, with progress being shown on the right of the screen.
